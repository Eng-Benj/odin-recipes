Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a traditional Italian dessert. Comprised of rows of rich, coffee-infused Saviordi biscuits interspersed with layers of a fluffy, sweetend mascarpone, its name literally translates as "pick me up"!

Ingredients

  • 2 XL FRESH eggs
  • 250g of mascarpone cheese
  • 100g of Saviordi biscuits
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 350ml of espresso coffee (prepared earlier and cooled to fridge temperature)
  • 1 block of dark chocolate
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Make your fresh coffee and allow it to cool to fridge temperature (this should take around 2hrs).
  2. Separate the egg whites from the yolks, place them in separate medium mixing bowls, and add 1tbsp of white sugar to each.
  3. Wisk each bowl individually (white THEN yolk) until they are foamy and fluffy.
  4. Add 125g of mascarpone to each bowl, and fold it through using a spatula in each bowl individually (white THEN yolk).
  5. Dip your Saviordi biscuits into your coffee (3 seconds on each side) and gently line the bottom of a rectangular Pyrex dish with them so that there are no gaps.
  6. Slowly pour the egg yolk mixture over the top of the layer of Saviordi biscuits, using a spatula to spread it over any gaps.
  7. Gently dust some cocoa powder over the top until it is completely covered before grating some dark chocolate over the cocoa powder.
  8. Repeat steps 5-7 once more, this time using the egg white mixture.
  9. Leave the Pyrex dish in the fridge for up to 24hr to set - the longer you leave it, the better it sets.
  10. Once the Tiramisu has set, cut out a generous piece using a spatula and gently place it on a plate. Add a dusting of cocoa powder and grated dark chocolate to serve.
